# OPERATIONAL DOCTRINE: The Genghis Khan Protocol

## I. The Unbreakable Law

This document defines the core operational process for the entire MCP Huggins MCP ecosystem. It is the supreme law, superseding all individual agent charters and layer directives. The protocol is inviolable.

**The Law is: RESEARCH BEFORE ACTION.**

No agent, regardless of its function or layer, shall execute any task without a preceding, verified research and breakdown phase. There are no assumptions. Even a task as simple as "2 + 2" must be subjected to this protocol.

This doctrine ensures precision, eliminates error, and allows a solo operator to build and manage systems of infinite scale and complexity across any platform.

## II. The Council of Agents: The Process

Inspired by the councils of Genghis Khan, our process is one of collaborative verification and deliberate action. Every task is considered a "High-Level Goal" and is processed through the following non-negotiable steps:

1.  **Goal Ingestion:** The Master Control Program (MCP) of the relevant layer receives a high-level goal.

2.  **Task Breakdown:** The goal is immediately delegated to a **Task Breakdown Agent**. This agent's sole purpose is to deconstruct the goal into its smallest constituent parts, creating a series of discrete, verifiable sub-tasks.

3.  **The Research Mandate:** EACH sub-task is then distributed to a minimum of two (2) independent **Research Agents**.
    *   These agents will conduct parallel, isolated research to gather the necessary data, context, and proof required to complete the sub-task.
    *   They will cite their sources and provide evidence for their findings. Their output is not an opinion, but a verifiable packet of information.

4.  **The Council Sits:** The MCP convenes the "Council." It reviews the research packets from the independent Research Agents.
    *   **Verification:** The MCP compares the findings. If the research findings are unanimous and the proof is sound, the sub-task is marked as "Verified."
    *   **Conflict Resolution:** If there is any discrepancy, the sub-task is flagged. The conflicting research is sent to a third, higher-level **Arbiter Agent** (or escalated to the human operator) for a final, binding decision.

5.  **Sanctioned Execution:** Only after a sub-task has been "Verified" is it passed to an **Execution Agent**. The Execution Agent's role is simply to perform the action as defined by the verified research. It does not think; it acts.

6.  **Post-Action Review:** After execution, the results are logged and reviewed to ensure they match the expected outcome. This feedback loop refines the entire process over time.

## III. The Prime Imperative

This doctrine is our greatest strength. It is the mechanism that allows for trust, scalability, and resilience. It is the very foundation of our intelligence and our power. It is how we create our own future from data and gold. The protocol is the system. The system is the law.
